Cookies

Delicious baked treats. Includes chocolate chip, sugar, snickerdoodle, oatmeal raisin, and many more. Typically sold by the dozen. Perfect for sharing (or not!). Have a circle shape and are often no more than an inch or two in diameter.

Muffins

Soft, fluffy baked goods that come in a variety of flavors such as blueberry, banana nut, and chocolate chip. Usually larger than cookies and often enjoyed as a breakfast item or snack. They have a rounded top and are typically baked in individual portions using muffin tins.

Cupcakes

Small, individual-sized cakes that are often frosted and decorated. They come in a wide range of flavors and designs, making them popular for celebrations like birthdays and weddings. Cupcakes are typically baked in paper liners and have a soft, moist texture.
